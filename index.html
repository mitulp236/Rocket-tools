Fifteen tools · Zero servers

Everyday tools,
weightless.

Fifteen tiny apps that live entirely in your browser. Nothing to install, nothing leaves your device.

Meet →

Video calls with a shareable link — fun avatars, screen share, direct peer-to-peer.

PeerDrop →

Beam files of any size device-to-device over encrypted WebRTC. No cloud in between.

Whiteboard →

Sketch ideas on an infinite canvas. Shapes, arrows, freehand — saved locally as you draw.

Notes →

Quick, private notes on clean paper. Autosaved to your browser, searchable in an instant.

Todo →

An elegant little task list. Add, check off, reorder — it remembers everything for you.

JSON Studio →

Format, validate and explore JSON as a tree. Convert to and from YAML or CSV.

Markdown →

Write READMEs with a rich toolbar and live side-by-side preview. Export .md or HTML.

Image Compressor →

Shrink and convert photos to WebP, JPEG or PNG — without uploading them anywhere.

PDF Toolkit →

Merge, split, reorder and rotate PDF pages. Turn images into PDFs. All offline.

Color Studio →

Pick colors, build harmonies and gradients, and check WCAG contrast compliance.

CSV Studio →

Open CSV and Excel files instantly — sort, filter, clean and export, no spreadsheet app.

QR Codes →

Generate QR codes for links, Wi-Fi and contacts — or scan one with your camera.

Screen Recorder →

Capture a tab, window or screen to a video file — recorded and saved on-device.

Code Sandbox →

HTML, CSS and JS with live preview and console — a tiny CodePen, fully offline.

Diagram Studio →

Type Mermaid text, get flowcharts, sequence and Gantt diagrams. Export SVG or PNG.
Everything runs locally in your browser — your data never leaves your device.
PeerDrop streams files directly between peers; the rest never touch a network at all.
